Our mission

We are building an event platform that behaves less like a static dashboard and more like an operations layer. The goal is to help teams automate repetitive tasks, coordinate quickly, and make better decisions with fewer tools.

From registration and venue planning to incident response and sponsorship tracking, EventOps AI keeps the work connected so teams can focus on delivering the event experience.

What the platform does

Registration Venue ops Speaker scheduling Sponsorship tracking Incident response Analytics

The result is a single place to understand what is happening and what needs attention next.

Who it is for

Event teams, operations leads, support staff, and administrators who need a clear view of registrations, sessions, resources, and live issues across one or many events.

  • Smarter decisions data-guided actions
